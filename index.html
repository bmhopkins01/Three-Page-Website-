San Fransisco

My favorite city in the U.S. is San Fransisco. I visited for the first time in 2016 with my freshman year roommate and a few other friends. We got to see Alcatraz, a couple of museums, and the beautiful Bay Area. The weather was fantastic and the hills reminded me of Athens. I would love to live there if it weren't so expensive!
